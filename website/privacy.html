PRIVACY POLICY

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 23, 2026

Fragment is an offline entertainment app for reading short mystery fragments. This policy explains how the app handles data.

Data Collection

Fragment does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share personal information.

Tracking

Fragment does not use tracking technologies, advertising identifiers, analytics SDKs, or third-party advertising services.

Network Access

Fragment does not require an account, login, external API, server connection, Firebase, or any network service for its core experience.

Local Data

The app may save simple local preferences on your device, such as favorite fragments or display state. This information stays on your device and is not sent to us.

Children

Fragment is not designed to collect information from children. Since the app does not collect personal information, no child personal data is knowingly collected.

Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact us through the support page.
